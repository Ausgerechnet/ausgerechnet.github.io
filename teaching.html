Teaching

I am currently conducting courses for the computational linguistics program at FAU (“Linguistische Informatik”). Teaching takes place in German. I also used to give various tutorials at the Department of Mathematics and the School of Business and Economics while I was still a student, and I used to give lots of private tuition in statistics (mostly for students of economics and business administration). In 2017, I was a Google Summer of Code mentor on behalf of the Distributed Little Red Hen Lab.
